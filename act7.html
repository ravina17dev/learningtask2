!! Dish !!

-Ingredients-

  • oxttail
  • banana flower bud
  • bok-choy
  • string beans
  • chinese eggplant
  • ground peanut
  • shrimp paste
  • garlic
  • onion
  • salt & pepper
  • water

:STEPS IN COOKING KARE KARE:

  1. : boil the oxtail: Put the oxtail in a large pot and add enough water to cover. Bring to a boil and skim off the foam/scum. Add chopped onion and crushed garlic. Simmer until the oxtail becomes very tender:

    Stove: about 2.5–3 hours

    Pressure cooker: about 40–50 minutes

    Keep the broth — this becomes the base of the sauce.

  2. : prepare the vegetables: While the meat cooks:

    Slice the Chinese eggplant into serving pieces. Trim the string beans. Cut the bok choy. lice the banana flower bud and soak briefly in salted water to reduce bitterness. Boil or blanch the vegetables separately until just tender: String beans: 3–4 minutes Eggplant: 4–5 minutes Bok choy: 1–2 minutes Banana flower bud: until tender

    Set aside.

  3. : make the peanut sauce:

    Once the oxtail is tender, keep about 4–5 cups of broth in the pot. Stir in the ground peanut gradually. Simmer for about 10 minutes while stirring occasionally. The sauce should become thick and creamy.

  4. : season it:

    Add salt and pepper to taste. Some people add a little shrimp paste directly into the sauce, but traditionally it’s served on the side.

  5. : comebine everything: add the cooked vegetables into the pot or arrange them on a serving plate. Pour the peanut sauce and oxtail over the vegetables.

    Serve hot with steamed rice and shrimp paste on the side.
