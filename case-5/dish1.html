Bicol Express

Bicol Express is a spicy Filipino dish made with pork, coconut milk, shrimp paste, and chili peppers. It is creamy, rich, and very spicy, best served with hot rice for balance. Traditionally, Bicol Express is served with hot steamed rice because the rice helps tone down the spiciness and lets you fully enjoy the sauce. It’s a comfort food for some, a challenge for others, and a signature example of Filipino cooking that highlights bold flavors and simple ingredients coming together in a powerful way.
