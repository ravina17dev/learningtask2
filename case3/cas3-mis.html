MISSION & VISION

MISSION

To form persons of integrity who are committed to the service of God and humanity through:

  • Holistic education that integrates faith, life, and culture.
  • Academic excellence driven by research, innovation, and industry alignment.
  • Social engagement focused on the poor, environment, and peace building.
  • Sustainable stewardship of all institutional and natural resources.

VISION

The University of St. La Salle is a premier Christian institution that envisions a society of fulfilled individuals who are socially responsible, globally competitive, and deeply rooted in their faith.
