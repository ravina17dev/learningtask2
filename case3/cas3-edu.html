University of Saint La Salle, Bacolod

The University of St. La Salle (USLS) is a premier Catholic private research university run by the De La Salle Brothers, located along La Salle Avenue in Bacolod City, Negros Occidental, Philippines.

The university offers a comprehensive range of programs across several foundational colleges

  • College of Arts and Sciences: Focuses on liberal arts, humanities, and social sciences with strong values formation.
  • Yu An Log College of Business and Accountancy: Hosts premium business tracks, recognized for generating top-tier entrepreneurs and accountants.
  • College of Medicine: A standout medical learning center dedicated to humane healthcare and social engagement.
  • The College Computing Studies is a premier tech hub in Bacolod City designed to train highly skilled IT professionals
  • The College of Tourism and Hospitality focuses on training students for global excellence in food and beverage services, culinary arts, and lodging operations.
  • Liceo De La Salle: The university’s specialized Senior High School department offering academic tracks for future success
