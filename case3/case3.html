University of Saint La Salle, Bacolod

The University of St. La Salle (USLS) is a premier Catholic private research university run by the De La Salle Brothers, located along La Salle Avenue in Bacolod City, Negros Occidental, Philippines.

The University of St. La Salle (USLS) is a premier Catholic private research university established on July 7, 1952, by three American De La Salle Brothers. Located in Bacolod City, Negros Occidental, it stands as the second oldest La Salle campus in the Philippines and the very first outside of Manila. Guided by its foundational motto "Religio, Mores, Cultura" (Religion, Morals, Culture), the university is widely recognized for its high licensure exam pass rates and its commitment to teaching minds, touching hearts, and transforming lives.
